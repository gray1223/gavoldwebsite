Grayson Bertaina

Owner, Pilot, Web Designer

About Me

My name is Grayson Bertaina. I started Gray AerialVisual in 2015 to do aerial photography and added web design soon after. I am fluent in HTML, CSS, and have my FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot License. I have done business with individuals and other businesses in our area and love to communicate. I am a musician and pilot (of actual aircraft), and will earn my Private Pilot license in the next month. I play the piano and baritone euphonium. I am also in Civil Air Patrol, where I am a Cadet Major and the Cadet Commander of the Ormond Beach Composite Squadron. I will graduate high school in the AICE diploma program in 2021 with my Associate of Arts Degree from Daytona State College and plan to attend the University of Florida to major in Aerospace Engineering.
