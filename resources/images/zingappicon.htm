Description

Drone delivery has never been easier. You can use this application to order food and medicine locally and have it delivered straight to your door by a drone today.


We use drone pilots from the vast network of over 170,000 Part 107 FAA Certified drone pilots throughout the United States.


This app is really easy to use. Just select a local business, select your items, and checkout. Join the drone delivery revolution today!


Features:

• Order from a variety of local restaurants

• Order prescriptions and over-the-counter medication from local phamacies

• Get notifications for order status updates

• Drone pilots will notify you when they are about to arrive

• Apple Pay accepted

• Quick and eco-friendly

• Login to see all of your past orders

• Rate businesses and check reviews
